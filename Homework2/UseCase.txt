1. Person goes to the gas station and parks car at the pump.
2. Person presses one of the payment options
3. Person fills tank with one of the three brands of gasoline: low grade, mid grade or high grade.
4. Person then pays using credit/debit card.
5. Person chooses whether to get receipt or not.
6. Person then leaves the gas station.

Variation #1:
1.1 In Step 2, Person chooses to pay with cash.
1.2 Person fills tank with one of the three brands of gasoline: low grade, mid grade or high grade.
1.3 Person goes inside the gas station to pay the cashier
1.4 Continue with step 5.
